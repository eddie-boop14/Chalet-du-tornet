# Chalet du Tornet

> Restaurant, bar and terrace at the heart of the Domaine du Tornet, on the shore of Lake La Balme-de-Sillingy (Haute-Savoie, France) — 20 minutes from Annecy. Generous traditional and Savoyard cuisine, a bar open all day, a terrace facing the water. Open February to early November.
